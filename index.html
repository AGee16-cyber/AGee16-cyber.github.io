Intro

Welcome! I'm a Data Analyst with a Bachelor's degree in Adult Education, bringing a strong foundation in analytical thinking and clear communication. My passion for understanding patterns and empowering informed decisions has led me to the tech space, where I leverage data to solve real-world problems. Take a look at my portfolio to see my approach in action. By the way, check out my awesome work.

Website Testing Dashboard

Analyzed an A/B test of two webpage versions to optimize user engagement. Utilizing Tableau for visualization, I focused on key metrics including time on page, bounce rate, clicks, and conversion rates. The analysis revealed statistically significant differences, leading to a data-driven recommendation to adopt Version A's layout and specific design elements. This project demonstrates my proficiency in A/B test analysis, user behavior interpretation, and providing actionable insights for website optimization.

Road Accident Dashboard

This project involved a comprehensive analysis of a Road Accident dataset to uncover critical trends, identify key contributing factors, and visualize patterns in road accidents in India. Utilizing Excel for data manipulation, as well as for visualization, I explored various data dimensions to understand the dynamics of accident occurrences. The analysis focused on identifying correlations between different variables (such as time of day, road conditions, vehicle type, etc.) and the frequency or severity of accidents. Key findings were visually represented to highlight accident hotspots, temporal trends, and the impact of contributing factors. This project demonstrates my ability to work with relational databases in this case, an Excel-based database, identify meaningful insights, and communicate findings effectively through data visualization.

Insurance Report Dashboard

This project involved a comprehensive analysis of policyholders, vehicle, and claim history data. The primary objective was to extract actionable insights to improve services, optimize premiums, and reduce claim costs. By applying data analysis techniques and leveraging Power BI for visualization, I aimed to identify patterns in claim frequencies and amounts, understand the characteristics of high-risk and low-risk policyholders, and provide a foundation for optimizing premium pricing models. The ultimate goal was to empower Insurance company to make data-driven decisions for targeted marketing strategies and overall business improvement within their auto insurance sector.

Order Fulfillment Dashboard

This project was all about looking closely at our delivery operations to figure out how we can make them faster and smoother. We used tools like Excel to put all our delivery information, like how many orders we get, how long deliveries take, and what customers think, into easy-to-read charts and tables.

Tenant Retention Dashboard

This project addresses the critical issue of tenant retention within a property management context. Recognizing the significant impact of tenant turnover on revenue and operational efficiency, this initiative leverages data-driven insights to identify key drivers of tenant churn and develop targeted strategies for improvement. The core of this project is a comprehensive Tenant Retention Optimization Dashboard, built to provide a holistic view of tenant satisfaction, lease management, and financial performance indicators. By analyzing key metrics such as the 50% Churn Rate, Average Satisfaction Score of 3.02, and alarmingly low 7% Rent Collection, we aim to understand the underlying factors contributing to these trends.
